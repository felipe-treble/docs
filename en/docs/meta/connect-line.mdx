## Requirements

Before connecting your WhatsApp line to Treble.ai, make sure you have:

- A verified Meta business portfolio.
- A Treble.ai account.
- A phone number that is not associated with any WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business account.
    - This phone number must be able to receive SMS or calls.

## Connect WhatsApp Line

To connect your WhatsApp line to Treble.ai, follow these steps:

1. Log in to your Treble.ai account.
2. Go to the **Settings** section.
3. Click on the **WABA** section
4. Click on the **Add phone number** button
5. Follow the instructions to connect your WhatsApp line.
