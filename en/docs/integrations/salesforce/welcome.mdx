Treble helps you harness the full potential of WhatsApp to sell more by integrating with Salesforce and AI. It allows you to automate lead interactions through AI chatbots and optimize communications that require human assistance through an AI-powered multi-agent system.

## Benefits

1. Improve first response time and automatically qualify leads
    - With an automated WhatsApp conversation, you can prospect in less time, nurture less qualified leads, and send only the most interested users to your agents.

2. Get metrics and optimize WhatsApp performance
    - Understand WhatsApp performance through clear reports and metrics, and optimize your use of the channel to increase your ROI.

3. Scale your Marketing and Sales operations with AI
    - Use Treble and give superpowers to your team. Scale your WhatsApp sales without needing to hire new salespeople.
